# Enemy Design — Kandra

*Shapeshifters with metal spikes. Can look like anyone.*

---

## Overview

Kandra are Hemalurgically created beings. They have a metal spine (the Blessing of Stability) and can change their appearance by consuming a body and wearing its skin. They're rare, dangerous, and nearly impossible to detect.

---

## Stats

| Stat | Value |
|------|-------|
| HP | 150 |
| Damage (Melee) | 35 |
| Speed | Fast |
| Detection | EXCEPTIONAL |

---

## Abilities

| Ability | Type | Description |
|---------|------|-------------|
| Shapeshift | Passive | Can look like anyone |
| Metal Eyes | Passive | See metal, including in darkness |
| Metal Sense | Passive | Detect nearby metal |
| Spike Strike | Melee | Attack with visible spikes |

---

## Behavior

### Undetected
- Walks among citizens
- Can pose as friends
- No warning before attack

### Detected
- Reveals spikes
- Aggressive pursuit
- Calls no backup (works alone)

### Combat
- Fast, aggressive
- Uses spikes as weapons
- Can see through darkness (tin eyes)
- Metal sight means hiding doesn't work

---

## Detection Method

| Normal Enemy | Kandra |
|-------------|--------|
| Vision | Metal eyes (sees metal) |
| Hearing | Hears everything |
| Stealth | Can't hide (they see metal) |

**Player can't use:**
- Dark corners
- Tin enhanced sight
- Metal hiding spots

**Player CAN use:**
- Silver weapons
- Non-metal hiding spots
- Coppercloud (hides Allomancy)

---

## Combat Strategy (For Player)

1. **Silver** — Only thing that hurts them
2. **Coppercloud** — Hides your metal
3. **Distance** — Run if no silver
4. **Ambush** — Kill before they reveal

---

## Silver Weakness

| Situation | Result |
|-----------|--------|
| Silver weapon hit | Major damage |
| Silver nearby | Discomfort, slows |
| Pure silver | Burns on contact |

**Why silver works:**
- Doesn't register as metal to their spikes
- Disrupts Hemalurgic connection
- Natural weakness

---

## Identity Mystery

Kandra can look like anyone. This creates narrative tension.

- Is that NPC really who they say?
- Trust issues
- The player might not know who to trust

---

## Visual Design

### Hidden Form
- Looks like normal person
- No visible spikes
- Blends in perfectly

### Revealed Form
- Metal spikes visible through skin
- Greyish skin tone
- Unsettling movement
- Glowing eyes

---

## Story Role

Kandra serve:
- The Lord Ruler's spies
- House assassins
- Secret agents
- Double agents
