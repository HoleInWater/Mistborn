# Level Design — Canal District

*The underground waterway system of Luthadel. Stealth-focused area.*

---

## Overview

The canals run beneath Luthadel, providing water to the city and secret routes for those who know them. Metal content is lower here (natural rock), making Allomancy less useful — but that's intentional. This area is about stealth, not flying.

---

## Atmosphere

- **Dark** — Minimal lighting, torches at intervals
- **Echo-y** — Water sounds, footsteps bounce
- **Wet** — Dripping water, puddles, mold on walls
- **Narrow** — Tight corridors, limited space
- **Metal-free** — Natural stone, few metal objects

## Metal Content

| Object | Mass | Notes |
|--------|------|-------|
| Grates (street) | 30 | Can be pushed |
| Chains | 5 | Pendants, decorative |
| Weapons (guards) | 2 | If surprised |
| Metal bars | 50 | Cage area |
| Gate mechanisms | 40 | Old, rusted |

**Low metal overall** — player must rely on stealth and physical skills here.

---

## Stealth Mechanics

### Sound
- Footsteps echo
- Water masks some noise
- Breathing is audible
- **Tip:** Crouch-walk in water

### Light
- Torches every 20m
- Shadows between lights
- Player has no light (can't use tin)
- **Tip:** Stay in darkness

### Sight Lines
- Narrow corridors = limited sight
- Cover points every 10m
- **Tip:** Use corners

---

## Enemies

### Patrol Routes
Guards walk the canal edges. Some carry torches.

| Guard Type | Weapons | Detection |
|-----------|---------|-----------|
| Canal Guard | Sword | Average |
| Tunnel Keeper | Crossbow | High |
| Kandra | Spikes ( Hemalurgy) | Unknown |

### Kandra (Special Enemy)

Kandra are shapeshifters with metal spikes. They can mimic people.

- **Detect metal:** See through tin eyes
- **Detect sound:** Track footsteps
- **Weakness:** Silver disrupts them
- **Combat:** Only if caught

## Key Features

### Water
- Chest-high in deep sections
- Slows movement
- Masks sound
- Metal objects rust quickly

### Old Machinery
- Gates that haven't worked in years
- Pulley systems (still work, metal)
- Flood controls

### Secret Areas
- Hidden alcoves
- Behind waterfalls
- Under walkways

---

## Player Approach

### Allomancer Playstyle
- Limited metal = limited abilities
- Can still push guards' weapons
- Can pull grate keys
- Must use physical skills too

### Stealth Playstyle
- Water hides footsteps
- Dark areas for hiding
- Environmental kills (drown guards)

### Mixed Playstyle
- Allomancy for key moments
- Stealth for traversal
- Save metal for boss

---

## Boss: The Keeper

Lives in the deep caves. Ancient Feruchemist.

| Ability | Type |
|---------|------|
| Zinc Cloud | Area effect, confusion |
| Coppercloud | Hide himself |
| Bendalloy Bubble | Slow time around him |

**Strategy:** Wait out his clouds, hit when visible.
