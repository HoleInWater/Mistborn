# Tutorial Design — Learning Allomancy

*A guided introduction to the game's core mechanics.*

---

## Overview

The tutorial should teach:
1. Basic movement
2. Allomantic Sight
3. Iron Pulling
4. Steel Pushing
5. Traversal with metals
6. Combining abilities

**Goal:** Player understands and can use Allomancy by the end.

---

## Location: Abandoned Warehouse

An empty warehouse on the edge of Luthadel. Owned by a friendly contact. Safe to practice.

### Features
- Concrete floor with metal grate
- Metal pillars
- Scattered metal debris
- One upper platform
- Exit to roof

---

## Tutorial Steps

### Step 1: Movement (2 minutes)

**Teach:** WASD movement, jumping, looking around

**Prompts:**
> "Use WASD to move around the warehouse."

**Check:**
- Player moves in all directions
- Player can jump
- Player looks around freely

---

### Step 2: Allomantic Sight (2 minutes)

**Teach:** Tab to activate sight, seeing blue lines

**Prompts:**
> "Press TAB to activate Allomantic Sight."
> "You see blue lines pointing to metal objects."
> "Thicker lines mean heavier metal."

**Check:**
- Player sees blue lines
- Player identifies metal objects
- Player can toggle on/off

---

### Step 3: Iron Pulling (3 minutes)

**Teach:** Left click to pull metal, relative mass

**Setup:** Metal coin on ground, player 5 meters away

**Prompts:**
> "Hold LEFT CLICK to pull metal toward you."
> "The coin flies toward you."
> "Heavier metal pulls YOU instead."

**Check:**
- Player pulls coin
- Coin comes to player
- Player understands mass rule

---

### Step 4: Steel Pushing (3 minutes)

**Teach:** Right click to push metal, flight basics

**Setup:** Coin on ground, player standing on it

**Prompts:**
> "Press RIGHT CLICK to push metal away."
> "Since the coin is light, it flies away."
> "But look — you're flying upward!"

**Check:**
- Player pushes coin
- Player moves upward
- Player understands Newton's laws

---

### Step 5: Anchored Push (3 minutes)

**Teach:** Push off heavy metal to fly

**Setup:** Metal pillar nearby, player on ground

**Prompts:**
> "That pillar is heavy — it won't move."
> "Push off it to fly in the opposite direction."
> "This is how you travel long distances."

**Check:**
- Player pushes pillar
- Player moves upward/forward
- Player can chain pushes

---

### Step 6: Combining Abilities (3 minutes)

**Teach:** Pull coin, push coin, repeat

**Setup:** Multiple coins scattered

**Prompts:**
> "Pull a coin to your hand."
> "Push it at that target."
> "This is your basic attack."

**Check:**
- Player pulls coin
- Player pushes coin at target
- Target takes damage

---

### Step 7: Free Practice (5 minutes)

Let player experiment. NPCs can offer hints if they're stuck.

**Options:**
- Try flying to the upper platform
- Practice coin combat
- Experiment with metal placement

---

## Summary

After tutorial, player can:
- ✅ Move around
- ✅ Use Allomantic Sight
- ✅ Pull metal (Iron)
- ✅ Push metal (Steel)
- ✅ Fly using anchored pushes
- ✅ Use coins as weapons

- Don't rush
- Let players experiment
- Hints appear if stuck for 30+ seconds
- Skip option for returning players
- Can replay tutorial anytime
