# Enemy Design — Koloss

*Big, strong, and stupid. Metal spikes through the body.*

---

## Overview

Koloss are Hemalurgically created soldiers. They're large, incredibly strong, and have metal spikes driven through their bodies. They're mindless — following simple commands but incapable of complex thought.

---

## Stats

| Stat | Value |
|------|-------|
| HP | 200 |
| Damage | 40 (melee) |
| Speed | Slow |
| Size | 8-10 feet tall |
| Detection | Audio only (can't see) |

---

## Abilities

| Ability | Type | Description |
|---------|------|-------------|
| Heavy Strike | Melee | Massive damage, knockback |
| Charge | Dash | Charges at player's last position |
| Metal Spikes | Passive | Can be pushed/pulled (weakly) |

---

## Behavior

- **Aggressive** — Attacks anything that moves
- **Stupid** — Falls for feints, charges into traps
- **Loud** — Makes noise, easy to lure
- **Blind** — Relies on audio, doesn't see
- **Strong** — Can break through weak walls

---

## Combat Strategy (For Player)

1. **Noise bait** — Koloss come to sounds
2. **Push them** — They can be pushed (metal spikes)
3. **Ranged attacks** — Stay at distance
4. **Environmental** — Push into pits, off ledges
5. **Tin immunity** — Can't be blinded, doesn't matter

---

## Weaknesses

| Weakness | Exploit |
|----------|---------|
| Blind | Move silently, use shadows |
| Slow | Kite around them |
| Stupid | Lure into traps |
| Metal spikes | Push/pull, steal spikes (future) |

---

## Visual Design

- Giant humanoid (8-10 feet)
- Greyish skin, scarred
- Metal spikes through skull, arms, chest
- Red eyes
- Tattered clothing
- Bulk of muscle but uncoordinated

---

## Spawning

Koloss don't patrol. They spawn in groups and attack.

| Scenario | Number |
|----------|--------|
| Random encounter | 2-3 |
| Boss encounter | 1 + minions |
| Koloss horde | 5-8 |
