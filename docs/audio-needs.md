# Sound Design — Audio Needed

*A list of sounds and audio we need for the game.*

---

## Allomancy Sounds

### Steel Push
- **Type:** Whoosh, force, impact
- **Length:** 0.3-0.5s
- **Mood:** Powerful, metallic, clean
- **Variations:** Light push (coin) vs Heavy push (anchor)
- **Priority:** HIGH

### Iron Pull
- **Type:** Sucking, pulling, magnetic
- **Length:** 0.3-0.5s
- **Mood:** Compelling, forceful
- **Variations:** Light pull vs Heavy pull
- **Priority:** HIGH

### Blue Lines Activate
- **Type:** Energy, hum, spiritual
- **Length:** 0.5-1s
- **Mood:** Ethereal, mysterious
- **Priority:** HIGH

### Metal Depleted
- **Type:** Empty, fading, exhale
- **Length:** 0.3s
- **Mood:** Disappointing, vulnerable
- **Priority:** MEDIUM

## Combat Sounds

### Player Hit
- **Type:** Impact, pain grunt
- **Length:** 0.2s
- **Mood:** Painful but not gruesome
- **Priority:** HIGH

### Enemy Hit
- **Type:** Impact, grunt
- **Length:** 0.2s
- **Mood:** Satisfying
- **Variations:** Different for different enemies
- **Priority:** HIGH

### Enemy Death
- **Type:** Groan, collapse, final
- **Length:** 1-2s
- **Mood:** Final, conclusive
- **Variations:** Skaa, Noble, Koloss, Inquisitor
- **Priority:** MEDIUM

### Coin Throw
- **Type:** Flick, coin spinning, release
- **Length:** 0.3s
- **Mood:** Quick, precise
- **Priority:** MEDIUM

### Metal Clang
- **Type:** Metal hitting metal
- **Length:** 0.5s
- **Mood:** Satisfying impact
- **Variations:** Light clang vs Heavy clang
- **Priority:** MEDIUM

## Notes

1. All sounds should have a slightly metallic quality (this is Scadrial)
2. Ash atmosphere should be constant background layer
3. Combat sounds need to be punchy but not excessive
4. Consider silence for stealth moments
