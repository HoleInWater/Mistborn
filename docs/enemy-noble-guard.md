# Enemy Design — Noble Guard

*Well-equipped soldiers in service to noble houses.*

---

## Overview

Noble Guards are elite soldiers hired by noble houses. They're better equipped and trained than Skaa soldiers, but still don't have Allomancy. They serve as mid-game enemies.

---

## Stats

| Stat | Value |
|------|-------|
| HP | 100 |
| Damage (Sword) | 25 |
| Damage (Crossbow) | 30 |
| Speed | Normal |
| Detection | Visual + Audio |

---

## Equipment

### Standard Loadout
- **Sword** — Primary melee weapon
- **Shield** — Blocks frontal attacks (50% damage reduction)
- **Armor** — Metal plate armor, can be pushed

### Elite Loadout
- **Crossbow** — Ranged attack
- **Throwing Knives** — Emergency ranged
- **Better Armor** — More HP

---

## Behavior

### Patrol
- Walks set routes
- Calls out time/status
- Responds to sounds

### Alert
- Investigates suspicious activity
- Calls for backup
- Raises alarm if player seen

### Combat
- Aggressive melee
- Uses shield to block
- Will call for help

---

## Combat Strategy (For Player)

1. **Disarm** — Push their sword away
2. **Shield Bypass** — Push from side or behind
3. **Range** — Use coins from distance
4. **Environmental** — Push into water, off ledges
5. **Stealth** — Kill before they alert others

---

## Weaknesses

| Weakness | Exploit |
|----------|---------|
| Heavy armor | Slow movement, can't climb well |
| Sword arm | Push weapon, they can't attack |
| Shield | Attack from side/behind |
| Noise | Alert others easily |

---

## Strengths

| Strength | Player Challenge |
|----------|----------------|
| Shield | Can't face them head-on |
| Backup | Quick kills needed |
| Armor | Lots of HP |
| Training | Good AI, doesn't fall for simple tricks |

---

## Variants

### House Venture Guards
- Red and gold colors
- More aggressive
- Better equipped

### House Penstock Guards  
- Blue and silver colors
- Defensive formation
- Better shields

### House Harwick Guards
- Purple and bronze
- Crossbow users
- Stay at range

---

## Visual Design

- Metal armor (can be pushed)
- House colors on tabards
- Professional appearance
- Well-maintained weapons
