# Mistborn Era One - Game Design Reference

*For Unity development - Era 1 timeline only (1022-1025 FE)*

## Quick Reference: The Sixteen Metals

### Physical (Push/Pull)

| Key | Metal | Effect |
|-----|-------|--------|
| RMB | **Steel** | Push metals away from you |
| LMB | **Iron** | Pull metals toward you |
| Q | **Pewter** | Strength, speed, healing, resistance |
| E | **Tin** | Enhanced senses (sight, hearing, touch) |

### Emotional (Mental)

| Key | Metal | Effect |
|-----|-------|--------|
| Z | **Zinc** | Riot emotions (make angry/scared) |
| X | **Brass** | Soothe emotions (calm) |

### Cognitive (Detection)

| Key | Metal | Effect |
|-----|-------|--------|
| C | **Copper** | Hide Allomantic pulses |
| V | **Bronze** | Detect Allomantic pulses |

### Temporal

| Key | Metal | Effect |
|-----|-------|--------|
| G | **Gold** | See your possible pasts |
| 5 | **Electrum** | See your possible futures |

### Noble Metals

| Key | Metal | Effect |
|-----|-------|--------|
| F | **Aluminum** | Purge all metals instantly |
| R | **Duralumin** | Burn all metals at once (mega burst) |
| 6 | **Chromium** | Negate enemy's metal reserves |
| 7 | **Nicrosil** | Amplify ally's metal burning |

### God Metals

| Key | Metal | Effect |
|-----|-------|--------|
| T | **Atium** | See enemy's next moves (ghost them) |
| Y | **Malatium** | See person's potential future self |

### Time Metals

| Key | Metal | Effect |
|-----|-------|--------|
| 8 | **Bendalloy** | Create time dilation bubble (speed up) |
| 9 | **Cadmium** | Create time dilation bubble (slow down) |

## Player Character: Vin

### Vin's Abilities (as of 1022-1025 FE)

**Core (can do from start)**
- Steel Push (RMB)
- Iron Pull (LMB)

**Learned During Era 1**
- Pewter Enhancement (Q) - learned early
- Tin Enhancement (E) - learned early
- Zinc Rioting (Z) - learned during First Empire
- Brass Soothing (X) - learned during First Empire
- Copper Cloud (C) - learned from Clubs
- Bronze Detection (V) - learned from Clubs
- Atium (T) - stolen from Elend
- Electrum (5) - after Atium runs out
- Aluminum (F) - known but rarely used
- Duralumin (R) - known but rarely used
- Malatium (Y) - near end of Era 1

### Vin's Combat Style

- Shadow wrestling background (street fighting)
- Trained by Kelsier in Allomantic combat
- Aggressive, fast-paced fighting
- Uses momentum and momentum flaring
- Later learns political/social combat

## Enemies

### Human Enemies

**Koloss**
- Hemalurgic soldiers
- Variable size (6-12 feet)
- Enhanced strength
- Blue skin when angry
- Limited intelligence
- Use any metal spikes for abilities
- Weakness: Coordination/leadership

**Noble Guards**
- Trained soldiers
- Have Allomancy
- Equipped with weapons
- Organized tactics
- Can have any metal abilities

**Steel Ministry Obligators**
- Priests/enforcers
- Pewter and Copper typically
- Grey robes
- Religious zealots

**Inquisitors**
- Hemalurgic constructs
- Near-immortal
- Multiple spikes
- Holes in skulls
- Hunt Allomancers
- Can regenerate wounds
- Superhuman strength

### Creatures

**Mistwraiths**
- Feed on Allomancers
- Mist-like creatures
- Lurk in caves
- Can consume metals
- Weak to Allomancy

**Kandra**
- Shape-shifters
- Take bodies
- Bound by contracts
- Can be allies
- Have Blessing spikes
- Intelligent

## Gameplay Systems

### Metal Reserves

Each metal has a reserve that drains when burned.

| Metal | Drain Rate | Special |
|-------|------------|---------|
| Steel | Medium | Push force scales with reserve |
| Iron | Medium | Pull force scales with reserve |
| Pewter | High | Drains faster in combat |
| Tin | High | Drains fastest when senses engaged |
| All others | Low | Passive drain when burning |

### Combat Mechanics

**Pushing/Pulling**
- Light metals fly toward/away from you
- Heavy anchored metals pull/push YOU
- Can push off metal buildings
- Can pull enemies with metal weapons

**Flaring**
- Briefly increase burn rate
- Massive power boost
- Quickly depletes reserves
- Used for finishing moves

**Alloying**
- Combine metals mid-flight
- Creates unexpected effects
- Advanced technique
- Example: Steel+Iron creates curved paths

### World State

**Pre-Well (1022 FE)**
- Lord Ruler alive
- Ash constantly falling
- Mists every night
- Nobles control cities
- Skaas enslaved
- Inquisitors hunting

**Post-Well (1024-1025 FE)**
- Lord Ruler dead
- Ash increasing
- Mists gone (protecting crops)
- Food scarce
- Koloss unchecked
- Political chaos
- Ruin manipulating

## Locations

### Luthadel

**City Layout**
- Capital city, ~400,000 people
- Outer districts (skaa)
- Noble keeps (inner walls)
- The Pits of Hathsin (nearby)
- Canton of Inquisition (center)
- Assembly Hall (government)
- Lord Ruler's Palace (destroyed 1022)

**Districts**
- **Theatern** - Noble district
- **Vander** - Commercial, skaa
- **Goves** - Noble, House Venture
- **Confiscreys** - Government buildings
- **Urteau** - Skaa slums, later burned

### The Pits of Hathsin

- Atium mines
- Near Luthadel
- Canton of Inquisition guards
- Prison labor (skaa)
- Koloss barracks
- Deep cavern system

### Kandra Homelands

- Beneath Dominant Mountains
- Network of caverns
- Sacred pools
- Kandra settlement
- Later invaded by koloss

## Factions in Era 1

### Luthadel Assembly
- Rules after Lord Ruler's death
- Nobles debating governance
- House Venture has military power
- House Elariel has political power

### Kelsier's Crew
- Led rebellion
- Took control of Luthadel
- Later formed new government
- Most members survived

### The Church of the Survivor
- Skeptic religion worshipping Kelsier
- Illegal but widespread
- Helps skaas
- TenSoon was member

### The Set
- Secret noble organization
- Preserve social order
- Existed since Lord Ruler's time
- Later revealed to be manipulators

## Victory/Progress Conditions

### Era 1 Game Goals

1. **Tutorial Phase**
   - Learn Steel Push
   - Learn Iron Pull
   - Escape abuse
   - Meet Kelsier

2. **Rebellion Phase**
   - Join the crew
   - Learn combat
   - Complete heists
   - Survive Inquisitor attacks

3. **Confrontation Phase**
   - Attack the palace
   - Face the Lord Ruler
   - Access the Well
   - Witness sacrifice

4. **Post-Victory Phase** (if continuing)
   - Defend Luthadel
   - Fight koloss army
   - Discover true enemy
   - Restore the world

## Audio/Visual Notes

### Sounds

**Allomancy**
- Steel push: Sharp metallic ring
- Iron pull: Deep resonant hum
- Pewter: Power surge, heartbeat
- Tin: Sensation of sharpness, visual crispness

**Combat**
- Sword clashes
- Koloss roars
- Inquisitor breathing (mechanical)
- Ash falling (constant)

### Visuals

**Pre-Well**
- Grey/ash sky
- Red/brown buildings (iron-rust)
- Dark nobility colors
- Blue metal lines (Allomantic sight)

**Mists**
- White fog at night
- Brighter when Preservation weakened
- Ruin's red tinge (later)

**Atium**
- Silver-white beads
- Glowing when burned
- Ghost images of future
