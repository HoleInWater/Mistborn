# Character Design — Vin

*Main playable character. Street rat turned Mistborn.*

---

## Background

### Early Life
- Born to a skaa family
- Mother killed by obligator
- Abused by noble stepfamily
- Learned to steal to survive
- Developed paranoia and mistrust

### Discovery
- Snapped during ashfall
- Revealed as Allomancer
- Recruited by Kelsier
- Learned the crew becomes her family

---

## Personality

| Trait | Description |
|-------|-------------|
| **Paranoid** | Always watching exits, expecting betrayal |
| **Survivor** | Does what it takes to live |
| **Loyal** | To those who earn her trust |
| **Hidden** | Keeps emotional walls up |
| **Dangerous** | Capable of violence when needed |
| **Vulnerable** | Longs for connection |

---

## Abilities

### Allomantic
| Metal | Skill Level | Use |
|-------|-------------|-----|
| Steel | Expert | Primary traversal |
| Iron | Expert | Combat, retrieval |
| Pewter | High | Combat mode |
| Tin | High | Sensing danger |
| Brass | Medium | Stealth missions |
| Zinc | Medium | Rioting enemies |
| Copper | Basic | Hiding in clouds |
| Bronze | Basic | Detecting threats |

### Physical
- **Agile** — Parkour, climbing
- **Stealthy** — Sneaking, hiding
- **Perceptive** — Notices details
- **Fast** — Quick reactions

### Combat Style
- Prefers range (coins)
- Uses Allomancy creatively
- Adapts to situation
- Will fight up close if needed

---

## Equipment

### Starting Gear
- Dark cloak
- Leather boots
- Coin pouch (100 coins)
- Small knife

### Upgrades (Later)
- Better Allomancy gear
- Noble-quality clothing
- Armored options
- Special items

---

## Visual Design

### Early Game
- Thin, underfed
- Dark, ragged clothing
- Hood up
- Covered in ash
- Street rat look

### Mid Game  
- Better clothes
- More confident posture
- Still practical
- Beginning to shine

### Late Game
- Noble-quality attire
- Confident bearing
- Mistborn power visible
- Leader appearance

---

## Voice/Tone

- **Quiet** when cautious
- **Sharp** when pushed
- **Soft** with trusted friends
- **Iron** when committed

---

## Arc in Game

1. **Tutorial:** Learning, following Kelsier
2. **Early:** Proving herself, building trust
3. **Mid:** Leading missions, making choices
4. **Late:** Becoming leader, facing destiny

---

## Player Connection

Vin is:
- Relatable underdog
- Powerful but not invincible
- Makes mistakes
- Grows throughout story
- Has strong supporting cast

**Goal:** Player sees themselves in Vin.
